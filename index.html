About us

  • We’re a Dominican-based enterprise called AJJ Purpose, which goal is to propose a better feminine clothing style in the Dominican Republic and the world. We strongly consider that the Dominican Republic is in a necessity for different clothing styles, and we want to change it. In order to do this we’ll spread our message to encourage Dominican women's costumer to invest in our high-quality dresses all around the world.

Our mission goes beyond simply offering high-quality formal dresses; we aim to revolutionize the feminine clothing style in the Dominican Republic and the global fashion scene. Recognizing the need for diverse and innovative fashion choices in the Dominican Republic, we are determined to bring about a transformation.
